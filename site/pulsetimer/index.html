PulseTimer

Haptic presentation timer for Wear OS.
Stay on pace without looking at your watch.

How it works

Create a timer pattern with milestones at the moments that matter. When a milestone hits, your watch taps your wrist with a distinct vibration. Add repeating interval signals to maintain a steady sense of pacing throughout your presentation, meeting, or workshop.

Features

Custom haptic patterns

Create distinct vibration signals for each milestone so you know exactly where you are in your talk.

Audio cues

Optional audio signals for environments where haptics alone aren't enough.

Live countdown

See elapsed time, remaining time, and a preview of your next signal at a glance.

Pause and resume

Pause mid-timer without losing your place if things go off-script.

Presets and custom patterns

Start with built-in patterns for common meeting lengths or create your own.

Watch face complication

Add PulseTimer to your watch face for one-tap launch before any meeting.

Background reliable

Runs accurately through screen timeouts and ambient mode transitions.

Fully private

No account needed, no internet required, no data collected. Everything stays on your watch.

Built for presenters

Whether you are giving a keynote, running a standup, facilitating a workshop, or leading a board meeting, PulseTimer keeps you in control of your time without being obvious about it. A subtle tap on the wrist is all it takes.
