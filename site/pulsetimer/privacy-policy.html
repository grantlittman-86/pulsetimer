PulseTimer Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 17, 2026

Overview

PulseTimer is a Wear OS application that provides customizable haptic and audio feedback timers for presentations and meetings. Your privacy is important, and PulseTimer is designed to respect it fully.

Data collection

PulseTimer does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app operates entirely on your device with no network connectivity required.

Local storage

Timer patterns and preferences you create are stored locally on your watch using Android DataStore. This data never leaves your device and is removed if you uninstall the app.

Permissions

PulseTimer requests the following permissions, all of which are used solely for core app functionality:

Vibrate: to deliver haptic feedback patterns during timers. Wake Lock: to keep the timer running accurately while your screen is off. Foreground Service: to maintain the timer when you navigate away from the app. Notifications: to display the ongoing timer notification.

Third-party services

PulseTimer does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's privacy

PulseTimer does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be posted here with an updated effective date.
