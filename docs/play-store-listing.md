## Full description

PulseTimer turns your Wear OS watch into a discreet presentation coach. Set up timed haptic vibrations and audio cues so you always know where you stand in a meeting, talk, or workshop, without ever glancing at your wrist.

How it works:
Create a pattern with milestones at the time markers that matter to you. When a milestone hits, your watch taps your wrist with a distinct vibration pattern. You can also add repeating interval signals (every 5 minutes, every 10 minutes) so you maintain a steady sense of pacing throughout.

Features:
- Customizable haptic patterns with distinct vibration signals for each milestone
- Optional audio cues for environments where haptics alone aren't enough
- Countdown display with elapsed time and next-signal preview
- Pause and resume mid-timer without losing your place
- Preset patterns for common meeting lengths (30, 45, 60 minutes)
- Create and save your own patterns for recurring events
- Watch face complication for one-tap launch
- Runs reliably in the background with ambient mode support
- No account required, no internet needed, no data collected

Built for anyone who presents, facilitates, or leads meetings and wants to stay sharp on timing without being obvious about it. Whether you are giving a keynote, running a standup, or keeping a workshop on track, PulseTimer keeps you in control.
